Biography

Hello! My name is Anna Blood and my skills are focused in math and science. I graduated from New College of Florida in 2019 with a Bachelors degree in chemistry, then from Emory University in 2021 with a Masters degree in organic chemistry. Now, I am in school to earn a second Masters degree in Computer Science from the University of West Georgia.

Tutoring

I have been working as a math and science tutor since high school.
